# 5pm AI

**Give Your Time Back.**

5pm AI is a practical AI workflow lab for AI explorers, engineers, architects, builders, and enterprise teams.

We believe AI should not make people work more.  
AI should help people finish better work, faster, and get their time back.

> Every day should end at 5pm.

## What Is 5pm AI?

5pm AI is not just a collection of articals, prompts, tools, repos or demos.

It is a long-term effort to make you learn more and build reusable, deployable, and practical AI workflows that help
individuals and organizations turn AI into real outcomes.

Our focus is on:

- Learning articals, resources sharing
- AI workflows for engineers and architects
- Enterprise AI adoption patterns
- Agent-based productivity systems
- Cloud-native and open-source AI tooling
- Practical examples that can be reused, deployed, and improved

The goal is simple:

**Build AI that gives people their time back.**

---

## Why 5pm AI?

Today, many people already use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, and other AI tools.

But most people still struggle with one important question:

**How do I combine these tools into a reliable workflow that actually saves time?**

5pm AI is built around that gap.

We care less about one-off prompts and more about repeatable systems:

- learning resources
- prompts
- agents
- MCP servers
- Cursor rules
- GitHub Actions
- cloud workflows
- enterprise knowledge bases
- AI-assisted SDLC patterns

The future of AI productivity is not just better tools.

It is better workflows.
